## Who we are and our role

[Operator] runs this instance of Parts. In data-protection terms, the therapist
using Parts is the *data controller* of their clients' information, and
[Operator] is a *data processor* acting on the therapist's instructions.

## What data we process

### Account data
Email address, username, display name, a hashed password, founding-circle
membership, and billing status (a paid-through date).

### Map content (clients' clinical data)
Entered by the therapist about their client: the Map's title; each Part's type,
label, description, somatic (body) location, free-text notes, and canvas
position; and the Relationships between Parts. This is special-category data and
is treated with the highest sensitivity. A full version history is retained.

### Technical data
Server logs (which may include your email address) and an encrypted session
cookie that keeps you signed in.

## How long we keep it

- Map content: for the life of the Map; deleted on erasure.

- Server logs: 30 days.
- Backups: a rolling 30 days — so erasure completes in backups within 30 days.
